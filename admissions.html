Courses Offered

Pre-School


Kinder 1 and Kinder 2
LCCV aims to provide toddlers with an environment that nurtures and enhances their learning and makes it fun, engaging and exciting. We aim to start a Godly education at an early age.

Requirements

  • Must be aged 5-6 years
  • 2 x 2 Colored Photos White Background
  • Baptismal Certificate



Grade School


Grade 1 to Grade 6
LCCV aims to nurture, enhance, and satisfy the innate cursiosity of children. The institution also aims to ready the students for higher learning that awaits them in the future.

Requirements

  • Must be aged 6 years or above
  • 2 x 2 Colored Photos White Background
  • Baptismal Certificate
  • Birth Certificate



Junior Highschool


Grade 7 to Grade 10
Make sure your kids are equipped to handle higher learning in a fun and exciting way

Requirements

  • Form 138(Report Card
  • Form 137(Entire Record)
  • 2 x 2 Colored Photos White Background
  • Certificate of Good Moral





Senior Highschool


Grade 11 and Grade 12
Choose from our offered strands you can take and make sure you are ready for college and beyond

  • General Academic Strand (GAS)
  • Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
  • Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM)
  • Home Economics (HE)


Requirements

  • Form 138(Report Card
  • Form 137(Entire Record)
  • 2 x 2 Colored Photos White Background
  • Certificate of Good Moral



College

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

  • Form 137 (Complete Records) - For Freshmen
